Our Mission



This program aims to provide a year-long immersive and accelerated learning for high school junior and senior students who have some IT experience to pursue further education and employment in IT fields.

Our Training



The program maps out training against the national IT Competency Model led by the Employment and Training Administration (ETA) 7 and includes preparation in three tiers:

1.

Technical Skills

Technical competencies that are core to the IT industry allowing workers to move easily across industry sub-sectors (fluidity)

2.

Employment Skills

Foundational competencies that focus on employability skills that are generally needed in the workplace

3.

Professional Network

Occupation-related competencies that tie to specific jobs which will be obtained through internship

Fun Fact !



We recruited 30 juniors and seniors from high school for the 2023-2024 cohort, investing over $4,000 in each student!
